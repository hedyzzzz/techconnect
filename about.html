Company History

TechConnect was established in 1974 by Mr Peter Griffin, trading from a single store in Quahog, Rhode Island, USA. He had one simple philosophy: to deliver a specialist range of Hi-Fi and recorded music at the lowest prices. TechConnect rapidly grew in Rhode Island and now has over 200 stores nationwide.

TechConnect moved to New Zealand in 2007 with the acquisition of Brian & Stewie. There are now 14 stores throughout the country and is New Zealand’s largest home and entertainment specialist.

With the original philosophy at the core of the business, TC stores offer the world's leading brands of computers, mobile phones, tablets, TVs, cameras, Hi-Fi, speakers, home theatre, portable audio and stacks more.

TC also offers the largest range of games, recorded music, DVD music + Blu-Ray and DVD movies and TV shows, all at cheap prices! Everything electrical you are after is available in one of our stores or online.

TechConnect has it all - best brands, huge range, cheapest prices, convenient locations, but most importantly genuine personal service from our experienced specialist staff..

The Executive Team

Peter Griffin

Chief Executive Officer

has a proven track record as one of Australia’s best retail executives and became Group CEO in August 2021. He was previously CEO of JB Hi-Fi from May 2010 to June 2014, following ten years as Chief Operating Officer, and oversaw significant expansion and growth during this time. Peter returned to the Group in April 2017 as Managing Director of The Good Guys and led the repositioning and significant improvement in performance of The Good Guys business.

Stewie Griffin

Chief Financial Officer and Executive Director

Stewie has been Chief Financial Officer of the Group since 2014, having joined the Group in 2009. Prior to joining TechConnect, Nick was a Manager at Deloitte where he provided Audit and Assurance services to a broad range of companies, including a number of Australian retail businesses.

Brian Griffin

Managing Director

Cameron has been Managing Director of JB Hi-Fi since October 2016, having joined the business in November 2009 as Merchandise Director. Between August 2018 and July 2019 he was the Managing Director Group Merchandise of the JB Hi-Fi Group, a short term appointment to assist the group to leverage its buying scale and processes across both JB Hi Fi and The Good Guys. Prior to joining JB Hi -Fi, Cameron worked for the Coles Group for 29 years holding senior roles in Store Operations and Merchandise Buying, including leading the Grocery and Fresh Foods Buying teams. Cameron is currently a Director of Narta International, Australia’s largest Buying and Retail Services company and he is also a Member of the Australian Institute of Company Directors.

Meg Griffin

Group HR Director

Meg was appointed Group HR Director in June 2019 and leads strategic initiatives to support our people objectives, including our focus on diversity and inclusion. Joining the Group in 2016, Meg was HR Director for The Good Guys, supporting the business through significant change including integration of TechConnect and The Good Guys. Previously, Meg held senior HR and Sales leadership roles in the energy industry.

Chris Griffin

Group Supply Chain Director

Chris has been Group Supply Chain Director since May 2016. Chris has worked at the JB Hi-Fi Group for over 15 years, during which time he has led Online, Commercial and Supply Chain. Chris was previously Chief Operating Officer at Catch of the Day, and has led key functions at EY, UBS and Symbion.
